Getting to our office

The Kelly Law Firm is located on the second floor of the Costal Towers at suite 211. Adjacent to the Costal Towers is a parking garage with guest parking for your conveince. From the parking garage, take the elevator to the base level and follow the walkway to the main entrance. Once you arrive at the main entrance, take the elevator to the second floor and follow the hallway to our offices at suite 211. We look forward to seeing you soon.

Location

The Kelly Law Firm is conveniently located in Fort Lauderdale’s iconic Costal Towers in North Fort Lauderdale located at the intersection of Commercial Boulevard and Federal Highway. It’s a mile away from Fort Lauderdale’s world class beaches and twenty minutes from Fort Lauderdale International Airport.
